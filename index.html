Bitrise on the go.

Builder is the easiest way to use Bitrise on iOS. Keep yourself up to date with your running builds, trigger new ones, and more.

Latest builds in a glance

All your latest builds at a glance, right after launching the app.

Browse your projects

Get access to every one of your projects, whatever type it is.

Start new builds

Trigger new builds in just a few taps. Select a branch, eventually a workflow, and voilà.

Download artifacts

If your workflow generates an .ipa artifact, download and install it right on your device.
